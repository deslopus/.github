# Code of Conduct

Deslopus is for practical, constructive work on improving AI-assisted software development.

## Expected Behavior

- Be professional, specific, and respectful.
- Critique ideas, code, prompts, and results rather than people.
- Assume published work should be inspected, tested, and improved.
- Make room for disagreement while keeping discussion useful.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained bad-faith argument.
- Publishing private information, secrets, or credentials.
- Using project spaces to pressure, spam, or derail maintainers or contributors.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent bans from Deslopus projects.

Report conduct concerns privately to an organization maintainer through GitHub.
